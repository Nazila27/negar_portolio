about me

We are a group of stylists

Negar is a pro makeup artist, hairstylist, and influencer with over 20 years of experience, with academic beauty certificates from Dubai, Turkey, Iran, and Canada. Negar is a skilled hairstylist; she does impressive haircuts and different looks.

Although bridal makeup always has been her most popular look, she is also into creative makeup and glamour. She is working with famous beauty brands such as Clarins, Armani, Estee lauder....and famous companies such as Hello Fresh, Rdstyles, Goli, Amazon as an influencer. She is also a freelance makeup & hair artist willing to travel.
